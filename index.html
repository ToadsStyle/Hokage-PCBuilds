How It Works

  1. Submit a build request through the form
  2. I review your parts list and confirm compatibility
  3. You ship the parts to me
  4. Parts are inspected when they arrive
  5. You receive an invoice for the remaining balance
  6. I build, test, and configure your PC
  7. Your PC is safely packed and shipped back to you
